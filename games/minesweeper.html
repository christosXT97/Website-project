Minesweeper

Uncover all the cells without detonating any mines. Use logic to reveal safe spots!

How to Play

  • Click on cells to reveal them.
  • Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag mines.
  • Reveal all safe cells to win.
  • Clicking a mine ends the game.
